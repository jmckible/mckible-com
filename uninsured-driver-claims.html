UNINSURED DRIVER CLAIMS

Having a car accident is traumatic enough. Finding out the at-fault party is an uninsured driver is even worse. However, there may be automobile insurance coverage available to compensate you for your injuries; this is called Uninsured Motorist Coverage.

Typically, when you file an uninsured motorist personal injury claim with an insurance company, it steps into the shoes of the negligent driver and does not typically act in your best interests.

In the event of an accident with an uninsured driver, most of uninsured motorist policies will cover personal injuries without having the need to sue the other driver.

McKible Law Office will seek to obtain the compensation you deserve for your uninsured motorist claim. This is done by carefully examining the details of the accident, any applicable uninsured motorist polices, and determining a fair settlement amount that you should receive from the uninsured motorist insurance company.

When you are injured in an accident involving an uninsured at-fault driver, having the right lawyer really does make a difference.
