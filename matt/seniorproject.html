This part of my site is dedicated to my senior project.

My senior project involves recording bands around venues in Indiana during their live shows. Over the last few months, I have recorded over a dozen bands and selected 10 songs to mix and master for a CD.

Every week I have been posting updated mixes to a SoundCloud. Below you will find them embedded.


For all of you audio people out there, here is a list of microphones/gear I used for all of these recordings:
  • Neumann KM 184 (overheads/misc.)
  • AKG C414 (guitars/misc.)
  • Shure Beta 52A (kick drum)
  • Shure SM 57 (snare/guitar)
  • Shure Beta 58A (vocals)
  • BSS AR 133 DI-box active (bass guitar/keyboard)
  • Toft ATB16 console
  • Avid 003 Rack
  • Pro Tools 8

The goal was to create live sounding songs that showcased the various band's natural sound.

Feel free to send me emails with questions or comments about any of these recordings.
